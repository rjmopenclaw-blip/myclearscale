Best Magnesium Supplements 2025

Magnesium is involved in over 300 biochemical reactions in the body — yet studies estimate 50–60% of Americans don't get enough. Poor sleep, muscle cramps, anxiety, and constipation are all common signs of deficiency. The problem is there are a dozen different forms of magnesium, and they're not all equally effective.

Key point: Magnesium oxide (the cheapest form, found in most drugstore vitamins) has poor absorption. Glycinate and malate absorb significantly better.
