Best Meal Replacement Shakes 2025

A good meal replacement isn't just a protein shake with extra calories. It should cover a meaningful percentage of your daily nutritional needs in a single serving — protein, carbs, fat, fiber, vitamins, and minerals. Most products on shelves don't come close. These ones do.

Who this is for: People replacing 1-2 meals per day for weight loss or convenience — without medication. Effective calorie control with full nutrition.

Meal replacement vs. protein shake — what's the difference?

A protein shake replaces the protein component of a meal. A true meal replacement replaces the whole meal — covering fat, carbs, fiber, vitamins, and minerals alongside protein. If you're using a shake to skip meals for weight loss, make sure it's nutritionally complete, not just protein-rich.
