Best Electrolyte Drinks 2025: LMNT vs Liquid IV vs DripDrop

Most electrolyte products are 90% sugar and 10% marketing. A few are genuinely formulated to replenish what your body loses through sweat, illness, or medications like GLP-1s. Here's what's actually worth buying.

Key difference: Electrolytes are sodium, potassium, and magnesium — not sugar. Any product leaning on sugar as the main ingredient is doing it wrong.

Top 5 Electrolyte Products Ranked

#1 — Best for Athletes & GLP-1 Users

LMNT Recharge

View on Amazon →
1000mg sodium
0g sugar
200mg magnesium

LMNT has the highest sodium content of any electrolyte product — which sounds alarming until you understand that sodium is the #1 electrolyte lost through sweat and the most commonly deficient on low-calorie diets. Zero sugar, clean ingredients. The Citrus Salt and Watermelon flavors are excellent. Particularly recommended for GLP-1 users experiencing fatigue, headaches, or muscle cramps.

#2 — Most Popular

Liquid IV Hydration Multiplier

View on Amazon →
500mg sodium
11g sugar
B vitamins

The best-selling electrolyte product in the US for good reason — it works, tastes great, and is widely available. The 11g of sugar is used intentionally (Cellular Transport Technology uses glucose to pull sodium into cells faster). Slightly lower sodium than LMNT but sweeter and more enjoyable for daily use. The Sugar-Free version is a good middle ground.

#3 — Best for Illness/Recovery

DripDrop ORS

View on Amazon →
330mg sodium
Medical grade
ORS formula

DripDrop is formulated to WHO oral rehydration solution standards — developed by a physician originally for emergency dehydration. It's the choice when you're sick, post-surgery, or dealing with GLP-1 nausea and vomiting. Not the best for daily use (lower sodium) but unmatched for acute dehydration recovery.

Drop a tablet in your water bottle and you're done. At 15 calories per tablet, Nuun is the most travel-friendly and convenient electrolyte option. Lower sodium than LMNT but adequate for moderate activity. The Tri-Berry and Lemon Lime flavors have strong followings. Good everyday option for people who want light hydration support without committing to a full powder.
