High Protein Foods: The Complete List

Most "high protein foods" lists just rank by total protein grams — which is misleading. A ribeye steak has a lot of protein, but also a lot of fat calories. We ranked by protein efficiency: grams of protein per 100 calories, which is what actually matters when you're managing your intake.

Protein efficiency score: grams of protein per 100 calories. Score of 10+ is solid. Score of 15+ is excellent. Score of 20+ is exceptional.
