What to look for (and avoid)

Green flags: Disclosed ingredient doses, third-party testing, organic certification, probiotics/enzymes included.

Red flags: Proprietary blends (hiding low doses), celebrity endorsements as the main pitch, 50+ ingredients but no doses listed, "$1 trial" subscription traps.
