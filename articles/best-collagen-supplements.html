Best Collagen Supplements 2025

Collagen is the most abundant protein in the body — making up skin, joints, tendons, and connective tissue. After 25, production declines. For people losing weight rapidly on GLP-1 medications, supporting skin elasticity through collagen supplementation has become a major topic — specifically in the context of "Ozempic face."

What to know: Collagen peptides (hydrolyzed) absorb far better than whole collagen. Look for Type I and III collagen from bovine or marine sources for skin and joint benefits.
