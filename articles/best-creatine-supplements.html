Best Creatine Supplements 2025

Creatine monohydrate is the single most studied supplement in sports science — with over 500 peer-reviewed studies confirming it works. It builds muscle, improves strength, aids recovery, and is increasingly studied for cognitive benefits. The only real question is which brand to buy.

Honest take: Creatine monohydrate is a commodity. The active ingredient is identical across brands — what you're paying for is purity, testing, and mixability. Expensive creatine isn't always better.

How to take creatine (simple protocol)

  • Dose: 5g per day, every day — timing doesn't matter much
  • Loading phase: Optional (20g/day for 5 days speeds saturation) but not necessary
  • With what: Mix in water, protein shake, or any liquid. Warm liquid dissolves it faster.
  • On GLP-1: Take it — muscle preservation on semaglutide is one of creatine's best documented use cases
