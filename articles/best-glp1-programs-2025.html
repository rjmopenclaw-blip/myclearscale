#3 — Hims & Hers: Most Affordable

From $79/mo — medication bundled in price

Hims & Hers offers compounded semaglutide at the lowest total cost of any provider we reviewed. The $79–$299/month covers everything — consultation, prescription, and medication delivery. No separate membership fee.

Best for: Budget-conscious people who primarily want medication access with basic medical oversight.

#4 — Noom Med: Best App Experience

From $59/mo + medication
See Noom Med →

Noom built its reputation on behavioral psychology — and Noom Med adds GLP-1 prescriptions to that foundation. Best-in-class app with daily lessons and a personal coach. If structure and data motivate you, Noom is worth it.

Best for: People who loved the original Noom app and want medication added on top.

#5 — Calibrate: Most Comprehensive

From $199/mo + medication (~$400–600/mo total)
See Calibrate →

Calibrate is the most medically rigorous program on this list — lab work, metabolic coaching, physician oversight, and a structured 12-month program. It's expensive, but it's a genuinely different product if budget isn't a constraint.

Best for: People with the budget who want the most rigorous medically-supervised approach.
