The 5 Best GLP-1 Programs in 2025 (Ranked by Value)

📅 Updated February 2025  ·  10 min read

Telehealth GLP-1 programs have made prescription weight loss medication more accessible than ever.

Not all GLP-1 programs are equal. The medication might be the same molecule, but the support, price, and experience vary enormously. We evaluated five major telehealth programs on: medication access, total monthly cost, quality of medical oversight, coaching, and ease of getting started.

#1 — Ro Body: Best Overall

From $99/mo + medication

Ro combines GLP-1 prescriptions (including semaglutide and tirzepatide) with lab work, ongoing physician messaging, and insurance coordination — all in one place. The onboarding is smooth, providers are responsive, and you're not left figuring things out alone.

  • GLP-1 + tirzepatide available
  • Lab work included in membership
  • Active insurance coordination support
  • Unlimited messaging with care team

Best for: People who want comprehensive medical support and are willing to pay for it.

#2 — Found: Best Value for Full-Service

From $49/mo + medication

Found is the best value among full-service programs. The $49/month membership includes behavioral health coaching, app-based check-ins, community support, and access to GLP-1 prescriptions. The coaching component is genuinely useful — not just a box-tick.

  • GLP-1 and non-GLP-1 medication options
  • Behavioral health coaching included
  • App with tracking, check-ins, and habit tools
  • Active community of members

Best for: People who want the medication plus accountability without paying Calibrate prices.

#3 — Hims & Hers: Most Affordable

From $79/mo (medication included)

Hims & Hers offers compounded semaglutide at the lowest total cost of any provider we reviewed. The $79–$299/month covers everything — consultation, prescription, and medication delivery. No separate membership fee.

  • Compounded semaglutide bundled in pricing
  • Discreet home delivery
  • Cancel anytime
  • No lab work or coaching included

Best for: Budget-conscious people who primarily want access to medication with basic medical oversight.

#4 — Noom Med: Best App Experience

From $59/mo + medication

Noom built its reputation on behavioral psychology for weight loss — and Noom Med adds GLP-1 prescriptions on top of that foundation. The app experience is the best in class. If you're someone who thrives with structure, daily check-ins, and data, Noom is worth the price premium over Found.

  • GLP-1 prescriptions available
  • Behavioral psychology-based program
  • Best-in-class app with daily lessons
  • Personal goal coach

Best for: People who loved the original Noom app and want medication added to their existing program.

#5 — Calibrate: Most Comprehensive (Premium)

From $199/mo + medication

Calibrate is the most medically thorough program on this list. Lab work, metabolic coaching, physician oversight, and GLP-1 prescriptions — plus a structured 12-month program designed around metabolic health, not just weight loss. It's expensive, but it's a different product than the others.

  • Comprehensive lab work included
  • Metabolic health coaching (not just weight)
  • Year-long structured program
  • Strong outcomes data

Best for: People with the budget who want the most rigorous, medically-supervised approach.
