Best Omega-3 & Fish Oil Supplements 2025

Omega-3 fatty acids (EPA and DHA) are among the most evidence-backed supplements available — with research supporting cardiovascular health, inflammation reduction, brain function, and joint health. The problem: most fish oil products are underdosed, oxidized, or sourced poorly. Here's what actually meets the clinical standard.

Effective dose: Most research uses 1,000–2,000mg combined EPA+DHA per day. Read the supplement facts carefully — "1,000mg fish oil" often only contains 300mg EPA+DHA. The rest is filler oil.
