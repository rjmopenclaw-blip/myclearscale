Why Hims & Hers Looks So Cheap

Hims & Hers bundles the consultation fee into the medication cost — so the $79–$299 you see covers both. It's compounded semaglutide, which is the same active ingredient as Wegovy but manufactured by a compounding pharmacy at significantly lower cost.

The tradeoff: no lab work, less medical oversight, no coaching. For someone who just wants access to the medication and is otherwise healthy, it works. For someone who wants comprehensive care, Ro or Calibrate is worth the premium.

Does Insurance Cover Any of This?

Rarely for weight loss. Here's the breakdown:

  • Ozempic for diabetes: Usually covered if you have a type 2 diabetes diagnosis
  • Wegovy for weight loss: Covered by some employer plans and Medicare Part D (as of 2024 for cardiovascular risk) — but far from universal
  • Compounded semaglutide: Almost never covered — but it's also 70–80% cheaper than brand name

Ro and Calibrate both offer insurance coordination support, which is worth using if there's any chance your plan covers it.

The Cheapest Legitimate Path to GLP-1

  1. Start with Hims & Hers — cheapest access to compounded semaglutide with medical supervision
  2. Try your insurance — use Ro or Calibrate's coordination support to check coverage
  3. If you want coaching + medication, Found at $49/mo is the best value
