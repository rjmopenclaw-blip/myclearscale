Best Supplements to Take With Ozempic

GLP-1 medications like Ozempic and Wegovy are highly effective for weight loss — but eating significantly less means you're also getting significantly fewer nutrients. Combined with the metabolic effects of rapid weight loss, most people on semaglutide develop predictable nutritional gaps that supplements can directly address.

Note: Always consult your prescribing physician before adding supplements. This is general guidance based on commonly reported deficiencies — not medical advice.

Why GLP-1 users need supplements

Three things happen when you're on semaglutide that create nutritional gaps:

  • You eat less — fewer calories means fewer vitamins, minerals, and protein
  • Muscle loss accelerates — rapid weight loss on GLP-1 includes muscle mass loss, not just fat
  • Absorption changes — slowed gastric emptying affects how well you absorb certain nutrients, particularly B12
